Life on My Wavelength

Greetings! I am currently in my fourth year pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, focusing on Web and Development, proudly representing the College of Information and Computing Studies at Northern Iloilo State University (NISU).

Although my academic journey began unexpectedly and presented numerous challenges, it has turned out to be the right path, leading me to a sense of fulfillment I wouldn't have discovered otherwise.

My life strikes a balance between my academic pursuits and my faith; I have been actively involved with the EL Shaddai DWXI Partners Fellowship, which is recognized as the largest Catholic charismatic movement in the Philippines.

I flourish in environments filled with positive, high-energy individuals, where I can openly express my true self without fear of judgment and engage in meaningful growth.

I am naturally warm and approachable, and I always fond of using humor as a means to connect with others. I have a lively personality and can easily adjust my style to resonate with those who share my joyful outlook. This ability to seek out and radiate positive energy shapes my personal approach, ensuring I surround myself with people who are on my wavelength.

Achievements

Milestones that shaped my journey

Academic Excellence

I may not have as many accomplishments as others due to my lack of self-confidence. However, I have reached a significant milestone in my life by being a consistent honor student throughout both elementary and high school. I graduated with high honors from Balasan National High School, where I was also honored to perform as one of the singers for our graduation song and tribute to our families. Now that I'm in college, I've kept up that consistent performance by being a Dean's Lister since my first year.

My Spiritual Home

My involvement as an active youth dancer and choir member is something I consider an achievement. Through music and dance, I've found ways to express myself, serve God, and connect with others on a deeper level.

Gaining experience Amid the Anxiety

I secured the position of second runner-up in the Mr. and Ms. CICS 2024 pageant during our College Week. I made an effort to face my fear and anxiety, aware that many people had faith in my abilities; however, I still felt unprepared for the experience. Nonetheless, I am grateful to God for this unique opportunity, which provided me with invaluable lessons.
